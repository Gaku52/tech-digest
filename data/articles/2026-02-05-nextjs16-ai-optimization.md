# Next.js 16 Announced with Built-in AI Optimization

## 概要

Vercel has announced Next.js 16, featuring native AI model integrations for edge functions. The release emphasizes optimizations for AI-driven apps. Engagements top 10,000.

## 詳細

### 背景
As AI becomes integral to web dev, Next.js evolves to support seamless integrations.

### 内容
New plugins for models like Llama, with auto-scaling for inference:
- **Native AI model integration**
- Edge function optimizations
- Auto-scaling for AI workloads

### 技術的詳細
Edge runtime now supports **WebGPU** for faster AI computations. Benefits include:
- Reduced latency for AI inference
- Better resource utilization
- Simplified deployment of AI-powered features

### コミュニティの反応
Enthusiastic adoption signals from full-stack devs.

## エンジニアへの影響

Integrate AI features to enhance app intelligence without third-party services.
